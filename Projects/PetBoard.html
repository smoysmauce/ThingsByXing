Interface Design

PetBoard

A group project in which we designed an application that helps pet owners monitor and stay connected with their pets whilst in spaceflight.

My Role:

Background & User Research

All background research for this project was done predominantly by myself. This included conducting scientific research on pets in space, as well as an online ethnographic study.

Interface Design

I was involved extensively in the design process of this interface, from the initial sketches, to wireframe mockups, to the final refinement of the interface.

Rapid Prototyping & User Testing

Our team collectively generated over 60 screens for our Petboard app, which were formed into prototypes. I also ran several user tests on our prototypes to assess their usability.

The Problem

As space travel looks to become more normalized within the next century, the possibility of bringing our beloved pets to distant planets should be thoroughly explored. With flights to Mars expected to take 80 days, services such as pet management and transportation must be reimagined to appropriately account for the hazardous environment of space.

Specifically, our team focused on the anxiety from pet owners that would arise from being separated from their beloved pets for over a few months as our key problem that would require a well-designed, empathetic solution, and so we got to work.

Our Solution

Assuming the introduction of these pet management services, we conceptualized an interface that would allow pet owners to monitor their pets in transit. This interface includes all the features needed to reassure pet owners that their pets are in safe hands and good condition.

Introducing PetBoard

Our final concept the “Petboard” aims to deliver complete comfort and reassurance for pet owners while their pet is travelling in space. Its functionality is shown below through the main screens.

01

Dashboard

Daily Update | Activity Log | Personal Shortcuts

Users are greeted with a stylish modern dashboard, containing quickly digestible, qualitative information. An inter-changeable shortcut bar is provided for users to be able to choose the data they want to see first.

02

Health and Nutrition

Health Data | Nutrition Report | Visual Diagrams

Health and nutrition data is visualised to allow users to easily interpret and assess the wellbeing of their pet. Each section is expandable, allowing for more in-depth, statistical data should the user require it.

03

Calendar

Upcoming Events | Past Reports

All the upcoming events for the user are shown here, whether it be health check-ups, approaching calls or video meetings. The layout is simple with an enlarged calendar and a sidebar with upcoming events.

04

Manager

Message Inbox | Booking Calls | Manager Profile

The manager interface allows users to communicate and stay connected with their onboard pet manager. On the left, it provides a profile and a brief description of the users personal pet supervisor. Users are also able to view the other managers that are onboard.
