Engage Youth KYCK2020 Shirts

A series of T-shirts created for the youth of Engage at St John's Anglican Church going into KYCK2020 (cancelled due to COVID-19). Designed to instil a sense of identity, both as members of Engage amongst other youth groups, as well as Christians amongst the public.
