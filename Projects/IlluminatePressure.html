Product Concept

Illuminate Pressure

A team project for the 2020 Biodesign Challenge, in which we conceptualized a pressure-sensing mattress topper designed to aid pressure sore patients. We placed top 6 amongst 44 universities around the world

See our website

Our team consisted of 6 design students, 2 medical science students and 1 architectural student. My main contribution to the project was, aside from the collective ideation and research of our product, all videos and presentations required for our project.
