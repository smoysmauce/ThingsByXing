About Me

Everyone has a desire to help others in some way. Mine is through design.

Hi, I'm Andrew, a passionate Design Computing student at the University of Sydney currently in my final year. Growing up, I was always more in touch with my creative side, and studying my degree has allowed me to pursue my passions in a practical manner.

I specialise in UX/UI and graphic design, though animation is a personal interest of mine. I wouldn't call myself a perfectionist, but when it comes to work, I'm someone who cannot submit a project that I wouldn't be happy with looking back.

Outside of design, I'm someone who loves jamming out, whether it be on the drums, piano, or guitar (which I recently picked up!). I'm also a huge Nintendo fan, and I highly doubt you'd be able to beat me in a game of Smash. In my free time you might catch me skating around Parramatta, which is also where I attend church.

Oh yeah, I love cats, especially mine. I think I have a bunch of photos of him somewhere on this site. See if you can find it!
