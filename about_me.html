About Me

Everyone has a desire to help others in some way.
Mine is through design.

Hi, I'm Andrew, a passionate Design Computing student at the University of Sydney currently. Growing up, I was always more in touch with my creative side, and studying my degree has allowed me to pursue my passions in a practical manner. I'm currently in my final year, and looking for graduate jobs where available!

I specialise in UX/UI and graphic design, though animation is a personal interest of mine. I wouldn't call myself a perfectionist, but when it comes to work, I'm someone who cannot submit a project that I wouldn't be happy with looking back.

Outside of design, I'm someone who loves jamming out, whether it be on the drums, piano, or guitar (which I recently picked up!). In my free time you might catch me skating around Parramatta, which is also where I attend church.
